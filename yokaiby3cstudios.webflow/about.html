ABOUT

Building communities.

3C Studios' first project in the Solana Blockchain, Yokai, aims to become an Arcade Club (YYC) with a special feature called Gamified Revenue Sharing. This incentivizes holders of the project. Yokai aims to always over-deliver: to the community, and for the community.

"Building Communities, within Communities..."

KITSUNE: YOKAI TOWER

The Kitsune DAO represents the Yokai Tower and holds exclusive access towards its revenue sharing model. Each round, 2 random cards will be drawn from a deck of 52 cards.

Your goal is to hope that the 3rd randomly drawn card's value would be in between the first 2 cards drawn.

Once you see the value of the first 2 cards, then you decide to double your bet if you think you have a big chance of winning or you can stay and continue to play the game with the amount you originally bet.

NOTE: When the 2 cards drawn are of the same value, you lose. If the 2 cards are consecutive, you win. (0.05, 0.1, 0.25, 0.5, 1 SOL entries)

KODAMA: GRID LOCK

The Kodama DAO represents the Yokai Minesweeper and holds exclusive access towards its revenue sharing model. Similar to the classic minesweeper game but with added spice: clicking on a Yokai Mask allows you to increase your SOL based on a compounding payout % per successful guess but clicking on a bomb makes you lose it all. (0.05, 0.1, 0.25, 0.5, 1 SOL entries)

KAPPA: FORTUNE WHEEL

The Kappa DAO represents the Yokai Fortune Wheel and holds exclusive access towards its revenue sharing model. There are 2 existing game modes: Win Solana or Win a NFT Token (Token to be used to claim the actual NFT).

AMABIE: YOKAI LOTTERY

The Amabie DAO represents the Yokai Lottery Club and holds exclusive access towards its revenue sharing model. 3 different lotteries: Grand (Weekly), Elite (Every 3 days) and Classic (Daily).

Grand Lottery - 0.1 sol entry, possibly win an NFT worth 150 SOL as the primary prize, secondary and tertiary prizes will also be available at a minimum entry of 2000 tickets.

Elite Lottery - 0.05 sol entry, possibly win an NFT worth 75 SOL as the primary prize, secondary and tertiary prizes will also be available at a minimum entry of 2000 tickets.

Classic Lottery - 0.025 sol entry, possibly win an NFT worth 40 SOL as the primary prize, secondary and tertiary prizes will also be available at a minimum entry of 2000 tickets.

DISCLAIMER: NFT Prizes stated below are relative to the amount of entries.

Yokai Yufuku Club offers four different factions: Amabie Club, Kappa Club, Kitsune Club & Kodoma Club. Each Club will have their own respective revenue sharing models depending on their assigned game mode. Click on each of the arcade machines above to learn more about all four factions!
